Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberris, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberris, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas
FEATURED IN: Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more than pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly slieed
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ourness brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, through it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberris

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter akk the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, through I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
