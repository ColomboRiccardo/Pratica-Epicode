Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Focaccia is undoubtedly the queen of all Italian leavened snacks, soft and fragrant, and it is amazing how you can achieve such a tasty yield using just a few simple ingredients.

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups
  • 1 1/2 teaspons
  • 1 1/2 teaspons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

For the toppings:

  • 4 cups
  • 1 1/2 teaspons
  • 1 1/2 teaspons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

Method

  1. Make the foccacia dough

    Let's start preparing the Focaccia Bonci let's put the flour, water the brewer's yeast in a bowl, I use the powdered one, if you use the fresh one let it dissolve in a little water and add it to the dough, mix with a fork so that the yeast is well dissolved and the water is incorporated into the flour.
